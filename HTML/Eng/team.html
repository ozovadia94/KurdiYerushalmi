Yaniv Ovadia

Vocals, saz, baglama & zurna

Comes from a traditional Kurdish Jerusalem family. Performing on vocals, saz, baglama and zurna. He is the director of the traditional Kurdish dance ensemble “Young Kurdistan”, director of “Kurdi Yerushalmi” and is an active member of the Kurdish community in Israel.

Eliyahu Dagmi

Leader in ethnic music scene

A leader in the ethnic music scene in Israel. Performing on guitar, and saz. He plays with Shlomo Bar, as well as dozens of Middle Eastern musical ensembles past and present ("Yona Ensemble", Barry Sochorof, "The Levis" and more).
